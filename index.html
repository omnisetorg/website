OmniSet is a script that automatically sets up your perfect Linux environment based on your needs - whether you're a developer, gamer, student, or content creator.
OmniSet works on most Debian-based Linux distributions including Ubuntu, Debian, Raspberry Pi OS, Linux Mint, Elementary OS, Pop!_OS, and Zorin OS.
